Tycoon Yachts was founded in 2021 as a yacht brokerage and servicing firm. We offer complete services to yacht owners who wish for a pleasant, hassle-free experience. With over a decade of industry expertise and understanding, we provide yacht owners with competent and dependable services. Our customer-focused staff strives to meet every client’s demand and expectation. We are happy to be your yachting experience's enduring partner.

We recognise that every yacht is a sophisticated and vital capital investment, and that the heart of yacht management is cost-effectiveness, risk mitigation, and regular quality maintenance. Each yacht is one-of-a-kind, as is each owner. We feel that each yacht necessitates her individual management program in order to meet client requirements. Each boat under our administration has a unique operation plan tailored to the owner's preferences. When calculating the yacht's budget, costs, and future resale value, we consider each owner's specific interests.


Due to the ever-changing legislation governing large yachts, we have a wealth of knowledge and expertise to handle the day-to-day operational challenges. We have worked with the most famous international yacht builders, dealers, suppliers, and specialists. With our meticulous expert services, we can relieve yacht owners of any stressful yacht management concerns and guarantee them a joyful yachting experience.

Under the supervision of our human resource professionals, we source the best available crew to make your yacht more than just a home. We never undervalue the significance of a special human connection.

Our team ensures that crew members are employed legally and in accordance with local and international law. Under the direction of the owner and in collaboration with the captain, we provide the full spectrum of crew management services.

From crew travel to crew training, employment agreements to payroll, you can rest assured that your crew manager will take care of all crew management requirements necessary for the efficient operation of your yacht.

We understand that most Owners have hectic schedules. To make yachting even more hassle-free for our honourable clients, other than traditional wire transfers, we also accept credit or debit card payments via Stripe.

Stripe, Inc. is an Irish-American financial services and software as a service (SaaS) company dual-headquartered in San Francisco, United States and Dublin, Ireland. It provides a safe and secure platform to make and receive payments for both customers and businesses.
